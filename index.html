Heineken

Heineken - Road to the Final

Heineken were looking for an innovative way to get more people engaged in their Road to the Final campaign. Many diehard football fans would compete and share pictures with their friends during the big matches, the battle ground was WhatsApp. We proposed and built a robot that would give the ultimate fans, the ultimate challenge for the ultimate prize... A chance to watch the Champions League Finals live in Lisbon.


Fans watching the game and enjoying a Heineken would send the word "Champions" to the Heineken Whatsapp number, after confirming they were above 18 they would be asked 5 random football trivia questions. Correct answers would automatically send them to the next question, 5 correct answers would add them to a draw where they stood the chance to win the trip of a lifetime.

Chase Bank - Customer care

Chase Bank Kenya has a solid reputation as one of most innovative banks in the country especially on Digital, the team at Chase bank had seen the exponential growth in Instant messaging and were looking for a way to offer support in this new space. Chase Bank was the first bank to use Whatsapp for Customer care, in just 3 months Whatsapp was already their number 2 customer care channel overtaking Email, sms and social media


Over 12 months they saw a significant reduction in their cost of contact and discovered that it was easier for their diaspora clients to contact and engage with them on WhatsApp.

CocaCola

Share a Coke demo

The Share a Coke campaign was a great success in Africa, it inspired us to run a weekend hackathon to answer the question " Can you share a Coke on Whatsapp ? " 48 hours later we had the answer. Using our custom API we built a robot that could receive a name via Whatsapp and automatically send you back a virtual Coke bottle with your name on it ready to share.


We shared this with a couple of friends to test it out. Within 2 days the secret was out and the numbers blew up, over 7000 virtual cans were created with over 1000 people messaging the robot on WhatsApp. We eventually had to shut down the experiment and only imagine how big it could have grown with some official endorsement.

Castle

Castle Lager - Selfies campaign

Castle in Tanzania ran many in pub promos, They were looking for a new idea, something more than just Facebook and Twitter, Ongair proposed an innovative campaign that took advantage of the huge WhatsApp community in Tanzania. During the promo revellers would send a code on the bottle to the Castle larger WhatsApp number, once the code checked out, they would automatically be prompted to send a selfie with their friends, proving that they were actually in the pub and enjoying a Castle lager. If they were in the same location as the activation team they would win instant Merchandise.


In the first 48 hrs of the campaign 2500 messages were sent and over 40 groups were formed, these groups continue to be activate months after the campaign ended still engaging with the brand and each other over a Castle.

Cheki - No. 1 in cars
